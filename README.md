# Performance-Logistics-Analysis
Conducted an in-depth logistics and performance analysis for a leading quick-commerce platform. The project focused on evaluating fleet efficiency, supply-demand dynamics, and delivery lifecycles across multiple operational pools to identify bottlenecks and optimize the last-mile delivery network.


Key Analytical Contributions:

Pool-Based Performance Breakdown: Analyzed delivery success rates and specific time metrics segmented by various operational delivery pools.

End-to-End Lifecycle Tracking: Mapped the complete order timeline to identify operational delays, measuring specific intervals including:

Time from Vendor Acceptance to Delivery Associate (DA) Acceptance.

DA wait times at the vendor facility.

Transit time from vendor to the final customer.

Supply vs. Demand Mapping: Calculated hourly order volumes to assess fleet supply against customer demand per pool, enabling better DA allocation and dispatching strategies.

Cancellation & Payment Correlation: Investigated the impact of different payment methods on order cancellation rates to provide actionable insights for minimizing revenue loss.

Trend Analysis: Performed comprehensive Week-over-Week (WoW) variance analysis on all core metrics to track performance shifts and operational improvements over time.

Skills & Techniques:

Supply Chain Analytics, Time-Series Analysis (WoW), Supply/Demand Forecasting, Root Cause Analysis.
